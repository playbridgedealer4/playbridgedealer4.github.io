How it works

Four Simple Steps to Efficient Card Dealing

01

Optical Recognition System

The PlayBridgeDealer4 features an optical recognition system that reads standard playing cards, including jumbo print and braille, ensuring accurate and swift dealing.

02

Durable Construction

Constructed with a stainless steel chassis and direct drive brushless motors, the machine offers durability and long-term performance.

03

Advanced Features

Frequent software updates, built-in diagnostic functions, and support from an international network of technicians ensure optimal performance.

04

User-Friendly Interface

The PlayBridgeDealer4 comes with a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to operate, even for beginners. The intuitive design ensures a smooth and hassle-free experience.

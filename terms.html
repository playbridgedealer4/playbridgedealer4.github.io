Terms and Conditions

1. RETURNS

All items leave our warehouse in perfect condition. If any item is found to be defective, we will give a full refund, or replace the item provided it is returned within 14 days of purchase. You may return unwanted items within 14 days and, provided the item is 'as new', we will refund you the purchase amount less a 15% handling charge and our outward postage.

2. CARRIAGE - see also Shipping Rates

Parcels of value up to £20.00 will be sent by standard post and will normally be delivered within 3-5 working days. Parcels of value of between £20.01 to £99.99 will be sent by 24 hour Courier Service if ordered before 3pm. Orders over £100 will be delivered free by the same service. 24 hour deliveries will be made Monday to Friday ONLY. Excluding Bank Holiday Mondays. Therefore, please order before 12noon on Thursdays for this service. We are able to arrange a Saturday delivery for an additional charge. If you wish to use this service you will need to contact us. Please note that 24hour delivery is NOT guaranteed.

3. DISPATCH

Orders will be dispatched on clearance of payment. If your order does not reach you within 10 days of dispatch notification, please notify us immediately or we may not be able to replace or refund your order.

4. WEBSITE IMAGES

Images appearing on our website are as a close a representation of our products as is possible. However colours may vary slightly, either from our suppliers, or if your computer monitor is not set up correctly.

5. STOCK AVAILABILITY

Our stock levels are normally maintained automatically. However, if we are unable to fulfil your order immediately, we will inform you by email and provide you with a likely delivery date. Stock levels are not maintained by, or updated to our online shop, so some items not be available as advertised.

6. VAT RATE

The VAT Rate now stands at 20% (as of 4th January 2011). All our prices include VAT.

7. HIDDEN COSTS

There are no hidden costs at our online shop. Packaging prices and VAT are included in the item price. Shipping Rates are viewable at the 'detailed item view' and 'checkout stages' and in our shipping rates table. The current rate of VAT is set at 20%. To make our prices fair and competitive, we have not added premium rate delivery charges to our item prices and then claimed free delivery. Our shop, therefore, works out the value of your total order and will assign the correct delivery charge to that order. This amount is shown at the checkout stage.

8. ORDERS FROM OUTSIDE MAINLAND UK

We have changed our shipping methods and prices in order to provide a fairer, more 'visible' and more competitive pricing system for all our customers. In order to provide the best prices, without adding premiums to each item we sell, in the name of "Free Shipping", we are now only able to provide an online ordering process to our UK mainland (including Isle of Wight) customers. Those from the Scottish Highlands and Islands, Isle of Man, Isles of Scilly, Northern Ireland, Republic of Ireland and all international customers, are invited to submit their requests by email or telephone, where a fairer, "best deal" quote will be returned to them, along with suitable payment options.

9. OTHER PAYMENT METHODS

We still accept cheques as a form of payment. If you wish to pay by cheque, please select cheque payment during the checkout process and follow the instructions. Orders will be dispatched on clearance of payment.

10. PHONE ORDERS AND PAYMENTS

We are still happy to take orders over the phone. Please have your item codes and quantities to hand, and payment can be made by Visa or Mastercard, or by business/personal cheque. Orders will be dispatched on clearance of payment.

11. VISITORS TO A.L.FLEMING

Visitors are always welcome to A.L.Fleming, but clients are reminded that we are a warehouse and not a shop, so please call before you visit.

12. PRIVACY POLICY

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998 and GDPR). We collect information about you for 2 reasons: firstly, to process your order and second, to provide you with the best possible service. We will not email you in the future unless you have given us your consent. We will give you the chance to refuse any marketing email from us in the future. The type of information we will collect about you includes: your name, address, phone number, email address, and credit/debit card details. We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent. The information we hold will be accurate and up to date. You can check the information that we hold about you by emailing us. If you find any inaccuracies we will delete or correct it promptly. The personal information which we hold will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy and the law. If we intend to transfer your information outside the EEA (European Economic Area) we will always obtain your consent first. We may use technology to track the patterns of behaviour of visitors to our site. This can include using a "cookie" which would be stored on your browser. You can usually modify your browser to prevent this happening. The information collected in this way can be used to identify you unless you modify your browser settings.

13. COOKIE POLICY

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to help us improve our website. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site. We use cookies to remember your preferences, to understand how you use our site, and to personalize your experience. You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

14. CHANGES TO TERMS AND CONDITIONS

We reserve the right to update or modify these terms and conditions at any time without prior notice. Your use of our website following any such change constitutes your agreement to follow and be bound by the terms as changed. For this reason, we encourage you to review these terms and conditions whenever you use our website.
